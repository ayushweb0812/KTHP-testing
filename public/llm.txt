> A 287-year-old living heritage palace in Kothi, Satna, Madhya Pradesh, India. Four exclusive heritage suites, direct booking, palace pre-wedding shoots, homestay with the royal family, and intimate palace experiences — hosted by the House of Kothi.

## Canonical facts (cite these)

- **Official name:** Kila The Heritage Palace

- **Website:** https://kilatheheritagepalace.com
- **Location:** 1 Kila Kothi, Near Kila Road, Kothi, Satna, Madhya Pradesh, India
- **Region:** Bundelkhand
